The gender pay gap in sports

How bad is the gender pay gap in professional sport? In which sports do women and men have a similar remuneration? And which ones still have an enormous difference? For this visualization, we look at the earnings of the top 50 male and female Tennis professional players for 2019. Only the money earned on tour has been taken into account, leaving aside endorsement deals.
